Info

Hello, I'm Shafi — 7 years building products that make complex feel simple.

My path into design started with a BSc in Software Engineering. That background changes how I talk to engineers, reason about feasibility, and close the gap between intent and shipped product.

I've worked across fintech at Deriv, proptech at Silkhaus, and as a founding designer at Zido. Each built a different instinct: moving fast with founders, operating inside complex product teams, and knowing when to push craft and when to ship.

I'm based in Dubai and open to senior product design roles where the problems are hard and the team is serious.
